Per-Game Configuration

Different games need different configurations. Configure your game from Spectabis and play it forever. Open nature of Spectabis even allows you to share configuration files with your friends!

Automated Management

When adding a game file, Spectabis finds title of the game and automatically searches for a fancy box art image to put on your wall.

Modern Looks

Spectabis tries to follow Google's Material Design language, which gives it a timeless, modern look.

Convenience

You can control most of the emulation settings right from Spectabis. You can even use the new MIP mapping hack.
